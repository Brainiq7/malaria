Ways to Use Garlic to Repel Mosquitoes Naturally

1. Eating lots of garlic is the simplest way to repel mosquitoes. It will be also good for health.
2. Make a mixture of garlic juice (one part) and water (five parts) and fill it in a spray bottle. Use this mixture to spray lightly on exposed body parts and keep mosquitoes away up to 5 to 6 hours.
3. Dip cotton cloth strips into garlic juice (one part) and water (five parts) mixture, then hang them in patios, decks or yard.
4. Rub garlic oil or raw garlic juice on your exposed skin to repel mosquitoes and prevent mosquito bites.
5. Consume garlic supplements regularly.
6. Just keep crushed garlic near the window to ward off mosquitoes.
7. Take 4 ounces of garlic bulbs and crush them using a meat tender. Put the crushed garlic into a small dish and add 2 tbsp of mineral oil. Leave this concoction overnight and in the next morning, add it to a pint of water. Fill in a spray bottle and apply to skin.
8. Make a mixture of garlic oil (1 part) and petroleum jelly & beeswax (100 parts). Apply it on the arms and legs to repel mosquitoes for up to 8 hours.

Warning:
Pregnant or lactating women and people consuming blood thinning medications should avoid garlic supplements.
